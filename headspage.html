I would want you to know that the school is very grateful for your continued support as this is key to the school progressing to another different levle and excellent service delivery .

Welcome to the official website of St Marys Secondary School. I am honored to serve as the Head of this esteemed institution and to share with you our commitment to providing a transformative educational experience for our students.

At St Marys Secondary School, we believe that every student has the potential to excel. Our dedicated team of teachers and staff is committed to nurturing each student's academic, social, and emotional growth. We offer a rigorous curriculum that is designed to challenge and inspire our students, while also providing them with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in higher education and beyond.

In addition to our academic programs, we offer a wide range of co-curricular activities that allow our students to explore their talents and interests. From sports and music to drama and debate, there is something for every student at St Marys Secondary School.

We are proud of the strong sense of community that exists at our school. Our students, parents, and teachers work together to create a supportive and inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and respected.

I invite you to explore our website to learn more about St Marys Secondary School and all that we have to offer. We are confident that you will be impressed by our commitment to excellence and our unwavering belief in the potential of our students.

Thank you for your interest in St Marys Secondary School. We look forward to partnering with you to help our students achieve their dreams.

Sincerely,

Mr. Nyabako
